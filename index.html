How Does This Work?

In today’s fast-paced world, you’ve got places to be and not enough time to get there. Meet Jetpack Rydes—an innovative flight service that gets you above the traffic and straight to your destination. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app: request, launch, arrive.

Download The App

Head to your app store and download Jetpack Rydes. You’re just a few taps away from your next jetpack ryde.

Request A Jetpack

We’ll get you airborne. Simply request a ryde in the app and we’ll connect you with a jetpack immediately.

Pick A Price

Choose the price you’re willing to pay and your ryde is set. The only surge is the acceleration you feel at liftoff.

Blast Off To Success!

After matching with your jetpack and confirming the details, you’re all set. Your jetpack will arrive shortly for launch.

Our Story

Jetpack Rydes was founded by a former hedge fund analyst and a software developer who shared a love of flight. After witnessing experimental jetpack tests over a Nevada sunset, they saw a chance to fuse aerospace engineering with modern apps—bringing personal flight to everyone. What began as a bold prototype became an on-demand platform for safe, fast, and unforgettable jetpack rydes. Today, Wild Rydes fields a growing fleet of meticulously tested jetpacks performing hundreds of flights each day.
