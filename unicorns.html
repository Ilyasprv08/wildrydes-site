Wild Rydes has a dedicated crew that designs, tests, and maintains our fleet of jetpacks. We take great pride in the quality of jetpacks and flights that we provide to our customers, and our staff exercises the utmost care in engineering and servicing each jetpack in our fleet.

Every jetpack goes through a rigorous testing process where we perform safety checks, flight simulations, and several rounds of stress tests. Jetpacks accepted to Wild Rydes are then treated to the best care and maintenance possible. We provide them with upgrades, premium fuel, and regular inspections. This is part of our company philosophy in which reliable jetpacks lead to happy customers.

Meet a few of the jetpacks that are part of our fleet.

ZoomBum 3000

White Turbo Series

ZoomBum 3000 joined Wild Rydes in February 2016 and has been flying almost daily. It’s known for fast takeoffs and smooth cruising altitude. In its downtime, ZoomBum enjoys cloud-chasing and watching air races.

Toasty Butt Booster

Black Heatwave Edition

This fiery jetpack has a reputation for high-thrust power. Originally built for stunt flights, it now spends its days impressing passengers with dramatic flame bursts and sharp climbs.

Air Fryer XL

Yellow Limited Edition

Air Fryer XL recently joined the Wild Rydes team in Madrid, Spain. With extra-large boosters and a love for long flights, this jetpack is a favorite among thrill-seekers who want to see the world from above.
